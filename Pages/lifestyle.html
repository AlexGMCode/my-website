Crafting and Gathering

Transform resources into might, magic, or market gold through FFXIV's deep crafting and gathering system. Whether you're harvesting minerals, fashioning armor, or brewing potions, every craft contributes to your journey. It's a whole world of creation waiting at your fingertips, offering not just utility but a satisfying experience of mastery.

Player Housing

Create your sanctuary in Eorzea through player housing! From decorating your personal space to hosting gatherings with friends, your home can be a place of rest, a trophy hall, or a vibrant social hub. Gardens, workshops, and more await, offering a personal touch to the vast world of FFXIV.

The Gold Saucer

Step into the dazzling Gold Saucer, where entertainment and excitement meet in activities like strategic Mahjong matches and heart-pounding Chocobo races. Whether you're gambling on a race, competing in mini-games, or enjoying the thrills of the arcade, the Gold Saucer promises endless fun and fabulous prizes.

Roleplaying

Join a welcoming community of roleplayers in FFXIV, where your character's story can unfold in any direction you choose. From casual gatherings in taverns to server-wide events, the roleplaying community offers a rich tapestry of collaborative storytelling and immersive experiences. Glamour

Express your style and personality through FFXIV's glamour system, the ultimate way to customize your appearance while keeping your competitive edge. Mix and match gear to create unique outfits, transforming battle armor into fashion statements. Whether you're attending a grand ball, exploring dangerous ruins, or just hanging out in the city, you can look your best and let your individuality shine. Glamour is truly the real endgame!

Island Sanctuary

Escape to your personal slice of paradise with the Island Sanctuary! This tranquil retreat offers a unique experience where you can farm, build, and care for animals at your leisure, all in a beautiful, secluded setting. It's a peaceful contrast to the adventurous life of an adventurer, providing a perfect place to relax, create, and manage your own island haven. Let your creativity and love for nature flourish in this idyllic escape.
