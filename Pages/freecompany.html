What are Free Companies?

In the lore of Final Fantasy XIV, Free Companies are mercenary groups established as an extension of the Grand Companies of the Eorzean Alliance. Following the Battle of Carteneau and the calamitous arrival of the Seventh Umbral Era, the Grand Companies suffered grave losses and needed to replenish their troops to ensure the safety of the realm. The Eorzean Alliance sanctioned the creation of Free Companies to bolster their forces and encourage the cooperation of adventurers. These organizations, established and operated by adventurers, serve as swift and efficient assemblies of troops against opposing threats.


In terms of gameplay, Free Companies are player-operated organizations that players can join or create. They function similarly to guilds or clans in other games, enhancing both the gameplay and the social aspects of FFXIV. Joining a Free Company is not mandatory, but it brings a wealth of benefits.

Benefits of Joining a Free Company

  • Company Chest - Members will be granted access to a company chest, which can be used to store items and gear to be shared with all members.
  • Company Actions - Free Companies offer Company Actions, which are buffs that can increase EXP gains, reduce travel costs, or buff stats across the entire roster.
  • Resource Pooling - A Free Company might also pool resources like gil and crafting materials in their Company Chest, contributing towards the FC’s goals.
  • Social Interaction - Free Companies provide a platform for players to socialize, often extending beyond the game to platforms like Guilded and Discord.
  • Shared Progress - If you’re interested in crafting and gathering, Free Companies might have members with professions that complement your own, allowing everyone to progress and reap the rewards together.
