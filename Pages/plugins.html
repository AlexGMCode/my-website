Dalamud Plugins

A collection of various plugins providing enhancements like UI improvements, additional in-game information, and quality-of-life features.

XIVCombo

Simplifies skill rotations by combining multiple abilities into a single button, reducing the number of hotbar slots needed and streamlining gameplay.

Simple Tweaks

Offers a collection of minor adjustments and enhancements to the game's UI and functionality, such as more detailed item tooltips and customizable party list options.

PennyPincher

Automates the process of entering gil amounts in market board transactions, allowing for quicker and more precise pricing adjustments.

ChatTranslator

Automatically translates chat messages between different languages in real-time, facilitating communication between players from diverse linguistic backgrounds.

MPTicker

Displays a customizable ticker for casters to monitor their MP regeneration in real-time, aiding in mana management during combat.

NoSoliciting

A spam filter that blocks RMT (real money trading) messages and other unwanted spam in chat, improving the chat experience.

XIVBars

Enhances the game's hotbar functionality, allowing for more flexible and visually distinct hotbar configurations.

Anamnesis

An advanced tool for posing characters and creating intricate screenshots or scenes, offering greater control over in-game photography.

Orchestrion

Provides an in-game music player interface, allowing players to easily select and play music from the game's soundtrack.

PingPlugin

Displays your current ping and latency in-game, helping identify connection issues or optimize network settings for better performance.

GatherBuddy

Offers assistance to gatherers, including notifications for when nodes become available and filters to find the most valuable gathering opportunities.
