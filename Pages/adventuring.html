Dungeons

Dive into the heart of adventure with your first steps into Sastasha, uncover the eerie secrets of Tam-Tara Deepcroft, and blast through the explosive depths of Copperbell Mines. Each dungeon in Final Fantasy XIV is a unique journey, filled with rich lore, challenging enemies, and epic loot. Perfect your skills and teamwork as you explore these iconic first dungeons!

Trials

Face off against the primal might of Ifrit, soar through the tempest against Garuda, and stand firm on the shaking grounds battling Titan. These high-stakes encounters will test your mettle and strategy, offering unforgettable moments and the thrill of victory against some of Eorzea's most formidable foes. Gather your allies and conquer these trials to become a true hero of the realm!

Raids

Step into the mysteries of The Binding Coil of Bahamut and the majestic Crystal Tower, where teamwork and strategy unlock the path to awe-inspiring vistas and lore-rich tales. These raids offer a blend of challenging combat, intricate puzzles, and stories that are key to the world of Eorzea. With epic rewards and secrets to uncover, these raids promise excitement and glory for all who dare to enter.

Hunts and FATES

Embark on thrilling hunts across the land to track down and defeat notorious monsters for grand rewards, or join in spontaneous FATEs (Full Active Time Events) for dynamic group battles against formidable foes. These activities provide a fantastic way to experience the vibrant, living world of Eorzea, meet fellow adventurers, and earn unique rewards.
