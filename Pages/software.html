Software for FFXIV

ReShade

ReShade is a powerful, open-source graphics enhancement tool that allows players to inject a wide range of visual effects into games, including FFXIV. It can significantly improve the game's visual appeal by adding features such as ambient occlusion, depth of field, and enhanced anti-aliasing. ReShade is highly customizable, enabling users to fine-tune the game's appearance to their liking or apply presets developed by the community to achieve specific looks or atmospheres within the game.

Advanced Combat Tracker

ACT is a complex tool that tracks combat metrics in games like FFXIV. It's primarily used to analyze and improve player performance by providing detailed statistics on DPS (damage per second), HPS (healing per second), and other combat-related metrics. While ACT is incredibly useful for players looking to optimize their gameplay, it's important to use it responsibly and in accordance with the game's terms of service, as some game developers may have restrictions on such tools.

XIVLauncher

XIVLauncher is a third-party utility for Final Fantasy XIV that offers features such as quick game starts, add-on support, and an in-game browser, enhancing the player's experience beyond what the official game launcher provides. It facilitates performance improvements and automatic updates for itself and its plugins, aiming to streamline gameplay and customization. However, as a third-party application, its use is unofficial and comes with risks, including potential violations of the game's terms of service.
