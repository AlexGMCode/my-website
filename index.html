About this page

Final Fantasy XIV has captivated players worldwide with its expansive world and engaging gameplay. Originally designed to be accessible on the PlayStation 3, its system requirements have evolved, yet it remains remarkably accessible. This site aims to help you not just run FFXIV on your PC, but ensure it performs optimally for an immersive gaming experience.
