Final Fantasy XIV is a captivating MMORPG that offers an immersive story, diverse job classes, engaging combat, crafting, and gathering systems, alongside rich social and cooperative gameplay, set in a visually stunning and expansive world.

Tanks, Healers, and DPS

In Final Fantasy XIV, tanks are the vanguards of any party, responsible for controlling enemy aggro and absorbing damage to protect their allies. Healers maintain the party's health through spells and abilities, ensuring that everyone can continue fighting by mitigating incoming damage and reviving fallen members. DPS (damage per second) roles focus on dealing damage to enemies, with specializations that allow them to excel in either single-target or area-of-effect situations, ensuring efficient combat progression.

Crafters and Gatherers

Crafters and gatherers in Final Fantasy XIV provide essential support to the game's economy and player progression by creating gear, consumables, and other valuable items. Gatherers supply raw materials needed for crafting and contribute to quests and levequests, while crafters can enhance gear, craft housing items, and create end-game consumables. Their combined efforts enable players to customize their gameplay experience, improve their performance in battle, and engage in the housing market and aesthetic customization of their characters and homes.

Scions of the Seventh Dawn

The Scions of the Seventh Dawn are a secretive group dedicated to ensuring the safety of Eorzea from existential threats, combining the efforts of two previous organizations, the Path of the Twelve and the Circle of Knowing. At the beginning of the A Realm Reborn expansion, they are at the forefront of the fight against the Garlean Empire and the primals, powerful beings summoned by the land's beast tribes. Led by Minfilia Warde, the Scions operate out of the Waking Sands in Thanalan, offering guidance, support, and intelligence to the realm's adventurers and city-states in these tumultuous times.
