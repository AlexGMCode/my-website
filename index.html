About Eorzea

Eorzea, a land embraced by gods and forged by heroes, comprises diverse regions each with its own unique character and history. Ul'dah, a bustling commercial hub, thrives in the deserts of Thanalan, its wealth matched only by the intrigues of its ruling elite. Limsa Lominsa, perched upon the cliffs of La Noscea, commands the seas with its formidable naval strength and pirate fleet. The verdant forests of the Black Shroud shelter Gridania, a sanctuary for those who heed the ancient whispers of nature. To the north, amidst the biting cold, lies Coerthas, a knightly realm where faith and valor withstand the ever-present threat of dragonkind. Together, these city-states form the heart of Eorzea, a realm of endless possibilities, shaped by the deeds of adventurers and the legacy of its past.

The story thus far...

In the annals of Eorzean history, the era leading up to the cataclysm known as the Calamity is marked by strife, heroism, and the indomitable spirit of its people. The land of Eorzea, rich in aether and blessed by the light of the Twelve, faced the resurgence of an ancient foe, the Garlean Empire, whose machinations threatened to tear the realm asunder. Amidst this turmoil, the Warriors of Light rose, champions foretold by prophecy, who alongside the Eorzean Alliance, stood against the darkness. Their deeds during these tumultuous times sowed the seeds of hope, but it was the unparalleled sacrifice of countless souls that ultimately forged the path to rebirth, ushering in the age of A Realm Reborn. This epoch is remembered as a testament to resilience and unity, a beacon for all who dwell within the renewed realm.
